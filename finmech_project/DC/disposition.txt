🧩 MAIN DISPOSITION GROUPS (SIMPLIFIED STRUCTURE)

Instead of 8 layers, all outcomes must fall under 3 master categories.

🥇 PRIORITY 1 — LEAD (SUCCESSFUL APPLICATION)

This is the highest priority outcome.

If the application process is successfully completed, then the disposition must always be LEAD, regardless of anything else earlier in the call.

Conditions for LEAD

Application is considered successful as LEAD if:

• If the Customer completes application and sees/gets Congratulations/Successful message.
• Application Shows or moved to under review on customer phone, confirmed by customer/agent.
•IF VKYC or BKYC completed successfully.
• Agent confirms card will be delivered in 5-7 working days.
• Customer confirms virtual card is visible in app.
• Card-to-card process completed successfully with Delivery time 5 to 7 days Exclamation by agent.
• Agent congratulates customer and confirms completion with Delivery time 5 to 7 days Exclamation.

##**Important Exception (Common Misunderstanding)**
If the app shows:

• “Thank you for applying the ICICI Credit Card”
• “Apply after 180 days”
• “Continue”

Or the Agent Only Thanks Customer but didn’t congratulates customer and confirms completion with Delivery time 5 to 7 days. Then The Customer is Not_Eligible

This means the customer is not eligible, even if the process was attempted.

➡ In this case the final disposition becomes NOT_ELIGIBLE.

• LEAD GENERATED BC WITHOUT REF CODE

🧠 DECISION LOGIC (SIMPLIFIED)

Evaluate the call using this order:

STEP 1 — Did the application complete successfully with Successful Message Confirmation and agent confirm the same with 5 to 7 days delivery time line?

YES → LEAD



STEP 2 — Did the application fail or not able to proceed further and agent says I will update the details and you will get a call later due to system / eligibility restriction?

YES → NOT_ELIGIBLE



STEP 3 — Did the customer refuse the card or show not interested behaviour and signalling with excuses?

YES → NOT_INTERESTED



🏆 GOLDEN RULE

Always determine what finally happened at the end of the call.

Priority Order:

1️⃣ LEAD
2️⃣ NOT_ELIGIBLE
3️⃣ NOT_INTERESTED

Higher priority outcome always overrides lower ones.
